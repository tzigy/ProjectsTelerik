About Just Puzzle

Just Puzzle is a browser game where you can spend your spare time solving puzzled images. You can choose from 2 options:

  • pick a image from the application gallery or
  • uploading your own image.

You can upload your own image, or choose one from our gallery.

Just Puzzle has 5 levels of difficulty you can choose (from separation 3x3 per row and column to 7x7).

After you choose image and level, press the SET button.

Ones image is shuffled, you can drag and drop the pieces in order to solve the puzzle.

Just Puzzle is running on Chrome, Firefox, IE, Opera or Safari.
